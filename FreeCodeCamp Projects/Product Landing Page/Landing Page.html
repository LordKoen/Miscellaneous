Introducing the new Surface Laptop 3

The new Surface Laptop 3 combines and improves on everything that makes the surface line-up so special. It now comes with 10th Gen Intel chips inside and a new selection of colours such as Sandstone and Black, which come in the new texture Brushed Aluminium.
Adding on the incredible, high resolution, touchscreen display that has always been of the strong points of the Surface line-up this years model comes with an improved trackpad and keyboard. Improving even further the premium feel of the machine.

Display

With a resolution of 2256x1504, the screen is sharp and kicks out plenty of brightness and decent contrast, with vivid colours. This means that this laptop is perfect for media consumption such as watching content and playing video games.
Another advantage of the Surface laptop 3 display is the 3:2 aspect ratio, which offers more vertical screen space when compared to other laptops with an 16:9 aspect ratio. This makes it perfect for getting work done and the screen really comes useful when doing various activities in this Laptop such as editing documents and photo editing.
To finish it off it also a touch screen support with stylus support, which means there are plenty of options to interact with This laptop, and one of them is bound to suit everyone's needs.

Keyboard, Trackpad and Speakers

Although this laptop offers many ways of interaction through the screen, it definitely does not forget about the Trackpad and Keyboard. This years version of the Surface Laptop 3 comes with an even bigger Trackpad, roughly 20% bigger than previous years, and with Windows precision drivers this trackpad is incredibly comfortable and accurate, setting an standard accross the industry.
The keyboard is just as impressive, offering a great balance between tactile feedback, travel distance and reliabilty. This creates a keyboard that feels incredible and comfortable to type on over long periods of time.
And to finish it off, the speakers on this laptop sound absolutely incredible coming from a 13 inch Laptop, the sound comes from beneath the keyboard which means that there are no speaker grills to be seen on this laptop creating an incredibly clean and premium feel.

USB C and Fast Changing

This years Surface Laptop 3 comes with a USB-C port, apart from the exisiting USB A. This means that are plenty of ways to connect to various devices such as monitors and docking stations. It is also capable of 60 Watt USB-PD Fast Changing, which means there are plenty of ways of ways to charge this laptop, going from 0 to 80% in abot one hour.
Another great thing about the Surface Laptop 3 is the combination of Windows Hello infrared facial recognition with the always-on technology which means that going back to work after a break is as simple as opening your laptop, apart from incredibly secure.
