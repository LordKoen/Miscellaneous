First Website

Welcome to Sinnoh :)

Hello ever yone, how are you guys?
A lot of exciting stuff has happened since my first post, I have been programming a lot, I have not learned much more since last time, however I had a chance to put those skills in practice creating my first webpage, which is a travel guide!

I did this as part of the tutorials I have been doing in Khan Academy. However I still tried to add my own personality to it, by making it a travel guide to the region of Sinnoh.

I love Pokemon, it is probably the franchise I have played the most and also enjoyed the most, one of the first Pokemon games I played is Pokemon Platinum and to this day it is one of my favourite Pokemon games.
I did not want to make this about a place that existed in real life, because I thought that was what everyone would do, so I decided to make it about the region in which Pokemon Platinum is set, to also remind me of the many reasons I love this game

I tried to use every bit of knowledge that I had with this website and I tried to make it decently detailed as by doing that I would be practicing more, here are some images of the website:

I am really happy with how the website turned out, it is quite basic but I think it looks really nice especially considering the fact that I have only been doing this for a few days and it makes me excited for what kind of stuff I will be able to do in the future with months and years of practice rather than hours and days
