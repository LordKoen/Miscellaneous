Pasta Carbonara:

  • Preperation Time: 15 Mins - 20 Mins
  • Cooking: 15 Mins
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Serves 4

    • Carbonara pasta is one of the easiest types of pasta a person can make, however this does not mean that it doesn't taste great. And after a bit of practice you can easilly make a delicious dish to impress someone or if you are simply not feeling like the same food for the 5th day in a row.

Ingredients:

    Ingredients Quantity
    Pancetta 100g
    Pecorino Cheese 50g
    Parmesan 50g
    Large Eggs 3
    Spaghetti 350g
    Plump Garlix Cloves 2
    Unsalted Butter 50g
    Sea Salt and Black Pepper As much as you want!

Step 1:
Put a large saucepan of water to boil.

Step 2:
Finely chop the pancetta and finely grate the pecorino and parmesan cheese. Mix them all together

Step 3:
Beat the eggs into a medium bowl and season with a little black pepper.

Step 4:
Add 1tsp of salt to the boiling water and the Spaghetti

Step 5:
Squash the peeled plump garlic cloves with a knife blade

Step 6:
Drop 50g of butter into a frying pan and as soon as the butter has melted, fry the pancetta and garlic

Step 7:
Cook until the pancetta is golden and crisp (about 5 mintues). Discard the garlic

Step 8:
Keep the Pancetta on low heat. Lift the Pasta from the water with tongs and put it in the pan with the pancetta.

Step 9:
Mix the cheese and eggs together, keeping some for sprinkling over later.

Step 10:
Take the pan of the heat and quickly pour the egg and cheese into it. Use the tongs to lift the Spaghetti and make sure everything is well mixed

Step 11:
Add some seasoning in the form of salt and black pepper (Optional Step).

Step 12:
Use a long-pronged fork to twist the pasta on to the serving plante or bowl.

Lasagna:

  • Preperation Time: 20 Mins
  • Cooking: 1 Hr and 40 Mins
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Serves 6

    • Lasagna is a classic dish which is prepared around the whole world. It can take many forms which means that everyone will like it in one way or another. However, today I am going to present you guys which a recipe for a classic Lasagna, and I will show how easy it is to make it. Another perk of this is that it can be frozen, and quickly unfrozen for a quick delicious meal at a later date!

Ingredients:

    Ingredients Quantity
    Olive Oil 2 tbsp
    Lean beef mince 750g
    Prosciutto 90g
    Tomato Sauce 800g
    Beef Stock (Hot) 200ml
    Fresh Lasagna Sheets 300g
    White Sauce 520g
    Mozzarella Strips 125g
    Grated Nutmeg As much as you want!

Step 1:
Heat 2 tbsp of olive oil in a frying pan and cook the lean beef in two batches for about 10 minutes each.

Step 2:
Finely chop 4 slices of prosciutto from a 90g pack, then stir through the mext mixture.

Step 3:
Pour the tomato sauce and the beef stock. Add the nutmeg, then season.

Step 4:
Bring up to the boil, the simmer for 30 minutes until the sauce looks rich.

Step 5:
Heat oven to 180C/fan/160C/gas 4 and lightly oil an ovenproof dish (about 30 x 20cm).

Step 6:
Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with lasagna sheets. Drizzle over roughly 140g of the white sauce.

Step 7:
Repeat step 6 until there are 3 layers of pasta and cover the top with the rest of the white sauce. Make sure to cover all the pasta.

Step 8:
Scatter the mozzarella over the top.

Step 9:
Arrange the rest of the prosciutto on top. Bake for 45 mins until the top is bubbling and lightly browned.

Beef Stroganoff:

  • Preperation Time: 10 Mins
  • Cooking: 35 Mins
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Serves 4

    • Beef Stroganoff is a favourite dish of mine, my mother used to always make it on special ocassions when I was younger which means that I was always really fond of it as I grew up.
    Although it can be eaten on its own, it can also be served in many ways including with rice or with pasta. It is also quite easy to cook so I am here to share this recipe. I really enjoy coding and cannot wait to go back to it

Ingredients:

    Ingredients Quantity
    Olive Oil 1 tbsp
    Sliced Onion 1
    Clove of Garlic 1
    Butter 1 tbsp
    Sliced Mushroom 250g
    Plain Flour 1 tbsp
    Sliced fillet steak 500g
    Creme fraiche 150g
    English Mustard 1 tbsp
    Beef Stock 100 ml
    Chopped Parsley As much as you want!

Step 1:
Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a non-stick frying pan then add 1 sliced onion and cook on a medium heat until completely softened, around 15 mins.

Step 2:
Crush in 1 garlic clove and cook for 2-3 mins more, then add 1 tbsp butter.

Step 3:
Once the butter is foaming a little, add 250g sliced mushrooms and cook for around 5 mins until completely softened

Step 4:
Season everything well, then tip onto a plate.

Step 5:
Tip 1 tbsp plain flour into a bowl with a big pinch of salt and pepper, then toss the sliced fillet steak in the seasoned flour.

Step 6:
Add the steak pieces to the pan, splashing in a little oil if the pan looks dry, and fry for 3-4 mins, until well coloured.

Step 7:
Tip the onions and mushrooms back into the pan. Whisk the crème fraîche, 1 tsp English mustard and the beef stock together, then stir into the pan.

Step 8:
Cook over a medium heat for around 5 mins.

Step 9:
Scatter with some chopped parsley, and serve with pappardelle or rice.
